About Platinum Media

Based in Belgrade, Serbia, Platinum Media is a premier creative agency dedicated to elevating athletes and sports brands through stunning visual content. We combine artistic excellence with a deep understanding of sports culture to create graphics and videos that resonate with fans worldwide.

Our passion for sport drives everything we do. From social media graphics that capture the intensity of competition to promotional videos that tell compelling stories, we help athletes and brands connect with their audiences in meaningful ways.
